Coquito Recipe

Coquito

Description

Coquito is a creamy, tropical, Puerto Rican holiday drink with coconut flavor

Coquito is usually served during Christmas and New Year's celebration in Puerto Rico

Ingredients

  • Evaporated milk : A can of evaported milk

  • Cream of Coconut : Canned cream of coconut

  • Eggs : Two beaten egg yolks

  • White rum : Puerto Rican rum is perfered, but, any white rum will do

  • Water : Water makes it super purable

  • Vannila : A teaspoon of vanilla extract

  • Spices : Cinnamon and cloves
